About MPOWER

MPOWER is a non-profit organization representing Black and minority professional masculine-presenting (lesbian) women and male-to-female (MTF) transitioned individuals across various industries.

Our purpose is to bring these like-minded and spirited individuals together to inspire, uplift, and motivate one another while still having fun. The mission is to create a supportive space where authenticity, strength, and unity thrive.

Structured like the President's cabinet, MPOWER is led by founder and President Tip White, with committee heads for Technology, Marketing, Treasury, Media, Events, and more. Each committee leads their own projects while also engaging in cross-functional roles to ensure the success of our collective goals.

Our Community Guidelines

We move with intention, respect, and power.

Stay active and engaged — online and in person.

Uplift each other: celebrate wins, offer support, show up.

Respect shared space, especially during guest events — allow room for everyone to connect and grow.

Inactive members may be removed to preserve the group's integrity and energy.

Lead with love, be accountable, and always come as you are.
